# Content Angle Prompt

This prompt is designed for AI-assisted content angle generation based on public trend signals, product research, customer pain points, marketplace patterns, and review insights.

It helps turn product opportunities into practical short-form video hooks, social media post ideas, SEO topics, ad angles, and newsletter content ideas.

Use this prompt only with public, legally accessible information. Do not use it to copy creators, clone competitor content, scrape private data, or generate misleading advertising claims.

---

## Prompt Name

```text
Content Angle Generator
```

---

## Purpose

Generate ethical and original content angles for product research, e-commerce marketing, social commerce testing, and trend report creation.

This prompt can be used for:

- TikTok content ideas
- Instagram Reels ideas
- YouTube Shorts ideas
- Pinterest content ideas
- Product page content angles
- Newsletter sections
- SEO blog topics
- Paid report content ideas
- Ad concept brainstorming
- Cross-border e-commerce content planning

## Hook Frameworks

### 1. Problem-Solution Hook

```text
If you struggle with [problem], this [product/category] may help you organize / simplify / improve [routine].
```

Example:

```text
If your necklaces always get tangled when you travel, this compact organizer may make packing easier.
```

---

### 2. Before-and-After Hook

```text
Before: [messy / frustrating / inconvenient situation]
After: [organized / easier / cleaner / calmer result]
```

Example:

```text
Before: jewelry tangled in a pouch.
After: rings, earrings, and necklaces stored in one compact travel case.
```

---

### 3. Routine Upgrade Hook

```text
A small upgrade for your [daily / travel / beauty / pet / home] routine.
```

Example:

```text
A small upgrade for anyone who travels with jewelry.
```

---

### 4. Gift Hook

```text
A thoughtful gift for someone who loves [interest / lifestyle / category].
```

Example:

```text
A thoughtful gift for someone who loves jewelry and weekend trips.
```

---

### 5. Mistake Avoidance Hook

```text
Stop doing [common mistake]. Try [better method] instead.
```

Example:

```text
Stop throwing your jewelry into a random pouch before a trip.
```

---

### 6. Hidden Problem Hook

```text
Most people do not realize [small problem] until [specific moment].
```

Example:

```text
Most people do not realize their jewelry storage is a problem until they are unpacking at the hotel.
```

---

### 7. Aesthetic Lifestyle Hook

```text
For the person who wants [routine / space / travel / beauty] to feel more organized and beautiful.
```

Example:

```text
For the person who wants their travel routine to feel a little more organized and beautiful.
```

---

### 8. Comparison Hook

```text
Instead of [old method], use [new method].
```

Example:

```text
Instead of stuffing jewelry into a makeup bag, use a compact travel case with separate compartments.
```

---

### 9. Buyer Identity Hook

```text
If you are a [identity], this is for you.
```

Example:

```text
If you are a jewelry lover who travels often, this is for you.
```

---

### 10. Low-Pressure Discovery Hook

```text
I found a simple way to [solve problem / improve routine].
```

Example:

```text
I found a simple way to keep small jewelry pieces organized while traveling.
```

## Platform-Specific Guidance

### TikTok

Best for:

- Demonstrations
- Before-and-after scenes
- Problem-solution videos
- Emotional storytelling
- Fast hooks
- Routine upgrades
- "Things I wish I bought sooner" style content

Avoid:

- Overly polished ads
- Fake testimonials
- Misleading claims
- Copying viral videos exactly
- Medical or guaranteed outcome claims

---

### Instagram Reels

Best for:

- Lifestyle visuals
- Aesthetic routines
- Beauty, home, fashion, travel, and gift content
- Brand-building content
- Soft selling

Avoid:

- Low-quality visuals
- Overhyped claims
- Too much text
- Copied creator formats

---

### Pinterest

Best for:

- Evergreen visual search
- Checklists
- Infographics
- Gift guides
- Organization ideas
- Aesthetic boards
- Seasonal content
- Blog traffic

Avoid:

- Low-resolution images
- Clickbait
- Misleading visuals
- Reusing copyrighted images

---

### YouTube Shorts

Best for:

- Quick demonstrations
- Product comparisons
- Problem-solution clips
- Mini tutorials
- Product testing content

Avoid:

- Unsupported claims
- Fake reviews
- Reposted content

---

### SEO Blog

Best for:

- Problem-solving articles
- Comparison guides
- Gift guides
- How-to content
- Category education
- Search-driven traffic

Avoid:

- Thin AI-generated content
- Unsupported statistics
- Copying competitor articles
- Over-optimized keyword stuffing

## Content Risk Guide

Flag these risks when relevant:

- Medical claim risk
- Wellness claim risk
- Financial claim risk
- Legal advice risk
- Baby or child safety risk
- Cosmetic or ingredient risk
- Electronics or battery risk
- Product safety risk
- Trademark or IP risk
- Copyright content risk
- Fake review risk
- Platform advertising policy risk
- Unrealistic result claims
- Before-and-after exaggeration risk
