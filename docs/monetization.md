# Monetization Guide

TrendPilot AI is not an auto-money system.

It is a toolkit for building ethical AI-powered trend intelligence workflows. The value comes from helping users collect public market signals, analyze product opportunities, generate useful reports, and make better research decisions.

This document explains possible monetization models for TrendPilot AI while avoiding misleading income claims, unsafe automation, spam, private data scraping, or platform abuse.

---

## Core Principle

TrendPilot AI should be positioned as:

```text
AI-powered trend intelligence toolkit
```

Not as:

```text
AI auto-money machine
```

Recommended messaging:

- Build trend research workflows
- Generate product opportunity reports
- Monitor public market signals
- Create e-commerce research templates
- Support product research decisions
- Help creators and sellers understand market trends

Avoid messaging:

- Guaranteed income
- Passive income without work
- Auto profit system
- No-effort money machine
- Secret platform loophole
- Automated scraping farm
- Spam lead generation system

## 1. Paid Trend Reports

### Description

Users can generate daily, weekly, or monthly trend intelligence reports based on public market signals.

Example report types:

- Daily e-commerce trend report
- Weekly product opportunity report
- TikTok product trend report
- Pinterest aesthetic trend report
- Amazon category research report
- Cross-border e-commerce opportunity report
- Country-specific consumer trend report

### Example Deliverables

A paid report may include:

- Top product ideas
- Source links
- Public trend signals
- Target audience notes
- Consumer pain points
- Product opportunity scores
- Content angle suggestions
- Risk and compliance notes
- Suggested next actions

### Pricing Ideas

Possible pricing ranges:

| Product | Example Price |
|---|---:|
| Single mini report | $5 - $19 |
| Weekly report | $9 - $49 |
| Monthly subscription | $19 - $99 |
| Category deep report | $49 - $299 |
| Custom research report | $199+ |

Prices depend on report quality, niche specificity, source quality, market demand, and human review.

### Important Disclaimer

Paid reports should not claim guaranteed sales, guaranteed profit, or guaranteed product success.

Suggested disclaimer:

```text
This report is based on public trend signals and AI-assisted analysis. It is intended for research and informational purposes only. It does not guarantee product demand, sales, profit, or business success.
```

## 9. Sponsorship and Donations

### Description

TrendPilot AI can receive support through sponsorship or donations.

Possible methods:

- GitHub Sponsors
- Ko-fi
- Buy Me a Coffee
- Open Collective
- Patreon
- Custom sponsorship links

### GitHub Sponsor Button

A repository can display a sponsor button using:

```text
.github/FUNDING.yml
```

Example:

```yaml
custom:
  - https://ko-fi.com/yourname
```

### Donation Positioning

Recommended wording:

```text
If this project saves you research time, you can support its development.
```

Avoid wording:

```text
Donate because this system will make you money automatically.
```

## Final Note

The best monetization path for TrendPilot AI is not hype.

It is trust.

Useful templates, clear workflows, ethical data practices, and honest limitations can make TrendPilot AI valuable to builders, sellers, researchers, and small teams.

The goal is to help people make better research decisions, not to promise effortless profit.
