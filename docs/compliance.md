# Compliance Guide

TrendPilot AI is designed as an ethical AI-powered trend intelligence starter kit.

This project helps users collect and analyze public trend signals, generate product research reports, and build market intelligence workflows.

It is not designed for spam, private data scraping, account farming, fake engagement, fraud, or guaranteed income automation.

---

## Core Compliance Principles

TrendPilot AI workflows should follow these principles:

1. Use public and legally accessible information only.
2. Respect website terms, platform rules, and applicable laws.
3. Do not collect private personal data.
4. Do not bypass login walls, paywalls, anti-bot systems, or platform restrictions.
5. Do not automate spam, fake engagement, fake reviews, or mass unsolicited messaging.
6. Do not claim guaranteed income, guaranteed sales, or guaranteed trend accuracy.
7. Keep source links and uncertainty notes for every important insight.
8. Use human review before publishing or monetizing reports.
9. Clearly separate original TrendPilot AI content from third-party tools.
10. Review third-party licenses before using external tools in production.

## Data Collection Boundaries

### Safe Data

Safe data usually includes:

- Public product titles
- Public product prices
- Public category names
- Public bestseller indicators
- Public review summaries
- Public article summaries
- Public trend report summaries
- Public search trend signals
- Public community discussion patterns
- Public news and industry updates

### Risky Data

Risky data includes:

- Usernames
- Personal social media profiles
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Private messages
- Private group content
- Buyer identities
- Seller private records
- Payment details
- Personal addresses
- Personal photos
- Health information
- Financial information
- Sensitive demographic data

TrendPilot AI should avoid collecting risky data unless there is a clear legal basis, explicit permission, and a legitimate use case.

For this project, the recommended default is:

```text
Do not collect private personal data.
```

---

## Public Source Checklist

Before using any data source, ask:

1. Is the page publicly accessible?
2. Is the content meant to be viewed publicly?
3. Does the source allow automated access or provide an API?
4. Does this require login?
5. Does this require bypassing a restriction?
6. Does the content include private personal data?
7. Can the information be summarized instead of copied?
8. Can the source be cited?
9. Is the data recent enough for the intended report?
10. Would this collection method look ethical if publicly disclosed?

If the answer is unclear, do not automate collection from that source.

## AI Output Rules

AI-generated reports should follow these rules:

- Do not invent facts.
- Do not claim certainty without evidence.
- Do not claim guaranteed sales or profit.
- Do not present weak signals as proven trends.
- Do not copy long passages from sources.
- Do not include private personal data.
- Do not generate fake reviews.
- Do not generate fake testimonials.
- Do not create deceptive advertising claims.
- Do not provide medical, legal, financial, or investment advice.

Every important trend insight should include:

- Source link
- Evidence summary
- Confidence level
- Risk note
- Suggested next action

---

## Confidence Levels

TrendPilot AI reports should label insights by confidence level.

| Confidence | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Low | Weak or early signal, needs more validation |
| Medium | Repeated signal from one or more public sources |
| High | Strong signal supported by multiple reliable public sources |

Important note:

```text
High confidence does not mean guaranteed sales.
```

It only means the research signal appears stronger than other signals.

## Copyright and Content Use

TrendPilot AI should not copy or republish third-party content.

Safe content behavior:

- Summarize in original language
- Cite source links
- Use short excerpts only when necessary
- Create original analysis
- Create original report templates
- Create original scoring models
- Create original workflow documentation

Unsafe content behavior:

- Copying full articles
- Copying full reviews
- Copying creator captions
- Copying product descriptions
- Reusing product photos without permission
- Reposting videos without permission
- Repackaging paid reports
- Removing author attribution

## Email and Messaging Rules

TrendPilot AI should not be used to automate spam.

Allowed:

- Generate internal report emails
- Send reports to opt-in subscribers
- Send alerts to your own team
- Draft outreach messages for human review
- Manage newsletter content for subscribers

Avoid:

- Mass unsolicited email
- Purchased email lists
- Scraped personal email lists
- Automated cold DM spam
- Fake personalization at scale
- Misleading sender identities
- Unsubscribe bypass
- Any behavior that violates anti-spam laws or platform rules

## Third-Party Tool Compliance

TrendPilot AI references external tools such as n8n, Firecrawl, Dify, Browser-use, and other open-source projects.

Users should:

- Read each tool's license
- Read each tool's documentation
- Follow each tool's terms
- Avoid copying source code without permission
- Avoid removing attribution
- Avoid implying official partnership
- Avoid combining incompatible licenses
- Avoid using tools for unsafe automation

TrendPilot AI does not provide legal advice.
