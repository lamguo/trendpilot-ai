This scorecard does not guarantee sales, profit, or business success.

## 5. Score Meaning

### Positive Factors

| Dimension | 1 Means | 5 Means |
|---|---|---|
| Demand Signal | Weak or unclear demand | Strong visible demand |
| Social Visibility | Hard to show in content | Easy to demonstrate visually |
| Price Potential | Low margin potential | Healthy margin potential |
| Supplier Availability | Hard to source | Easy to source |
| Differentiation Potential | Hard to improve or brand | Easy to improve, bundle, or brand |

### Negative Factors

| Dimension | 1 Means | 5 Means |
|---|---|---|
| Competition Level | Low competition | Very crowded market |
| Shipping Difficulty | Easy to ship | Large, heavy, fragile, or costly |
| Compliance Risk | Low risk | High legal, safety, platform, or certification risk |
